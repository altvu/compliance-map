# Modern Compliance Stack Map

An interactive visualization of how infrastructure, identity systems, and compliance workflows intersect — and where audit bottlenecks are created.

## Overview

Most organizations don’t struggle with *lack of compliance data* — they struggle with:

- Manual evidence collection  
- Fragmented systems (AWS, GitHub, Okta, Jira)  
- Repeated audit preparation cycles  
- Lack of continuous visibility  

This map visualizes how those breakdowns occur across:

- Infrastructure → Evidence → Workflow → Pain → Resolution

## What This Shows

- Where compliance evidence originates  
- Why manual collection becomes the bottleneck  
- How audit pain propagates across teams  
- Where orchestration (automation) resolves the problem  

## Technology Layer (CaaS)

In the final layer, “CaaS” (Compliance-as-a-Service) represents the orchestration layer that connects directly to your existing systems and resolves the workflow breakdown.

Platforms like ZenGRC sit in this category — acting as the layer that:

- Automates evidence collection  
- Maps controls across frameworks  
- Provides continuous audit readiness  
- Eliminates manual coordination across teams  

## How to Use

- Click through the steps to follow the compliance lifecycle  
- Hover over nodes to explore relationships  
- Trace the dominant path to understand root causes  

## Why This Matters

Compliance failures are rarely technical — they are **workflow failures**.

Understanding the system is the first step to fixing it.
